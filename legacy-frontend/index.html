How this supports E-Governance Viva

  • Decentralization: Votes are stored on an Ethereum-like blockchain (Hardhat local network).
  • Immutability: Once a vote transaction is mined, it cannot be altered.
  • Transparency: Results are computed on-chain via getResults().
  • Smart Contracts: Business rules (one-person-one-vote, time-bound election) are enforced automatically.
  • Extensibility: Same pattern can be extended for land records, fund tracking, and digital identity (DID).
